Soft computing

Soft computing constructs computationally intelligent methods by combining emerging problem solving technologies such as fuzzy logic, probabilistic computing and reasoning, artificial neural networks, evolutionary algorithms, and reinforcement learning to solve complex and real world problems. Various methods used in soft computing are neither independent of nor compete with one another, but rather, they work in a cooperative and complementary way. Soft computing is tolerant of imprecision, uncertainty, partial truth and approximation.
(Bhattacharyya, Dhruba Kumar; Kalita, Jugal Kumar. Network Anomaly Detection: A Machine Learning Perspective. CRC Press. 2014)
